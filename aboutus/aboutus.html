Research Laboratory of Medical Electronics
“LET Medical”

Research Laboratory of Medical Electronics “LET Medical” LLC is a patent owner and developer of medical devices and technologies of medication-free treatment — SCENAR-therapy, COSMODIC-therapy and ENS-therapy.

The Research Lab was founded in Taganrog in 1990 by Alexander Karasev, the inventor of technologies for medication-free treatment — SCENAR, COSMODIC, ENS.

“LET Medical®”, “ЛЭТ Медикал®”, “COSMODIC®”, “КОСМОДИК®”, “SCENAR®”, “СКЭНАР®”, “MYOSCEN®”, “МИОСКЭН®” “ENS®”, “ЭНС®”, — are trademarks and service marks registered by “LET Medical”.

The Research Lab specializes in development of electronic equipment for medical and research purposes and also state-of-the-art non-invasive medication-free technologies of treatment and health recovery.

At present the Research Lab works in the following fields:

  • R&D in the field of electrostimulation, electrotherapy, magnetotherapy, phototherapy, wave therapy, resonance therapy and development of new trends and applications for SCENAR, COSMODIC and ENS therapy and SCENAR, COSMODIC and ENS cosmetology;
  • development of new prospective methods of diagnostics, prophylaxis, rehabilitation and medication-free treatment;
  • development and production of devices implementing technology of SCENAR-therapy, COSMODIC-therapy, ENS-therapy;
  • development and production of hardware-software complexes, mobile diagnostic systems;
  • development of training programs and multimedia systems in the field of medicine, production of training aids for SCENAR-COSMODIC therapists — in printed and electronic form (electronic textbooks, etc.).

“LET Medical” Research Lab is the world’s leader in the field of technologies implementing SCENAR-therapy, COSMODIC-therapy and ENS-therapy.

“LET Medical” Research Lab has developed a unique COSMODIC technology, which has no analogues in the world. “LET Medical” is an exclusive manufacturer of devices implementing this technology.

Recent years have been dedicated to the work on development and implementation of new treatment technology — ENS-therapy.

“LET Medical” has representatives and medical centres in Russia, UK, Canada, Australia, USA, Sweden, Greece, Japan.

All inventions of “LET Medical” Research Lab are protected by patents in Russia and abroad.
